Public Safety Advisory

SCP-3008 is described as a retail unit whose interior leads to SCP-3008-1, a space resembling an IKEA store that extends beyond normal physical limits.

Priority guidance: Do not separate from your group, mark your route, conserve battery, identify shelter, and move before low-light periods.

Field Recognition Indicators

  • Entrance location cannot be reacquired after line-of-sight is broken.
  • Interior floorplan appears larger than the outside structure.
  • Repeated furniture departments, warehouse aisles, and staged living areas.
  • Human survivors forming makeshift settlements.

Emergency Procedures

Immediate Action
  1. Stop movement and account for all persons.
  2. Mark current location with visible, repeatable indicators.
  3. Identify water, food, defensible shelter, and low-light risks.
  4. Avoid unknown staff-like entities and hostile contact.
Night Cycle Safety

Relocate to elevated or barricaded shelter before lights-out. Maintain quiet, low profile, and redundant exits.

Group Discipline

Use buddy pairs, check-ins, written route logs, and basic incident command roles: lead, medic, supply, lookout, recorder.
